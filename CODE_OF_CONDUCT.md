# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

As contributors and maintainers of this project, we pledge to respect all people who contribute through reporting issues, creating pull requests, updating documentation, and other activities.

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of behavior that is not acceptable:
- Harassment, prejudice, or abusive comments of any sort
- Personal or political attacks
- Posting private or confidential information

If you are subject to or witness unacceptable behavior, please raise an issue and contact the maintainers directly.
